## Search intent
Commercial-informational. Wedding printable sellers (huge Etsy sub-vertical, avg listing price $8–35) need a reference for the correct print sizes across the full wedding suite: invitation, RSVP, menu, program, seating chart, welcome sign, table numbers, thank-you cards. Buyers search similar queries when they need to print at home or at a print shop. Sellers lose sales when they don't include the sizes buyers' printers expect.

## Why high-potential
- Wedding printables is one of the top 3 niches on Etsy for digital downloads (alongside wall art and planners).
- Multi-size bundling is EXACTLY the SnapToSize use-case: a wedding seller typically needs the same design output at 4x6, 5x7, A5, A6, 8.5x11, 11x17, 18x24, 24x36.
- Zero overlap with existing pages: we have `etsy-digital-planner-sizes` (planners) and many wall-art niches, but nothing covering the wedding stationery vertical.
- Strong conversion angle: seller pain = "I have to resize every template into 8 different sizes for invites + signs". SnapToSize ships this as one pack.
