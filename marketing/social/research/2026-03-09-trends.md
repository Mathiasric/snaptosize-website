## Instagram Trends

### Format Trends

**1. Carousels (still king for engagement)**
- Swipe-worthy structure (cliffhanger on slide 2)
- 5-7 slides optimal
- Bold text, high contrast (mobile-friendly)
- Last slide = CTA

**2. Reels with text overlay**
- No face required (screen recordings work well for SaaS demos)
- Trending sound MANDATORY for reach
- Hook in first 3 seconds (critical)
- 15-30 seconds optimal

**3. Educational content beats promotional**
- How-to guides, problem-solving content outperforms "buy my product"
- Example: "5 Mistakes Etsy Sellers Make" beats "Try SnapToSize"

### Hook Patterns (from TikTok, applicable to Instagram)
- ✅ "POV: You just discovered [solution]"
- ✅ "5 mistakes [target audience] make with [topic]"
- ✅ "Stop doing [common mistake]..."
- ✅ "Here's why your [thing] isn't working..."
- ✅ "[Number] tips for [goal]..."

**Hooks to avoid:**
- ❌ "Let me tell you about..." (too slow, people scroll)
- ❌ "This is a game changer" (overused, vague)
- ❌ No hook at all (instant scroll)
