## Top Opportunities (ranked by potential)

### 1. Landscape / Horizontal Print Sizes for Etsy
- **Search evidence:** Reddit thread (r/Etsy, 2024) — seller says "I was able to put up Portrait Prints just fine with the usual 4:5/3:4/2:3/11x14/5x7 ratios but I'm confused how to do this with Landscape images." Etsy marketplace has dedicated categories for "horizontal wall art size guide" and "wall art size guide landscape." YouTube video "14 Sizes from 4 Files" covers this. Facebook group post asking about landscape photography file sizes. Active Etsy market pages for "landscape art prints horizontal" and "printable wall art landscape panoramic."
- **Why it's a gap:** ALL our size pages and ratio content assume portrait orientation. We have zero content addressing landscape/horizontal sizes. This is a massive blind spot — every seller doing photography, landscapes, or panoramic art hits this confusion.
- **ICP fit:** Direct hit. Sellers creating landscape photography, panoramic art, or horizontal designs need to know: do you flip the ratios? What are the standard horizontal frame sizes? How does SnapToSize handle landscape images? This is a sizing question our tool solves.
- **Suggested slug:** /etsy-landscape-print-sizes
- **Page type:** guide (with size reference table)
- **Priority:** HIGH — addresses real documented confusion, no competitor has a definitive guide, directly showcases SnapToSize's orientation handling

### 2. Square Print Sizes for Etsy (1:1 Ratio — 8x8, 10x10, 12x12)
- **Search evidence:** Active Etsy marketplace categories for "12x12 square prints," "8x8 modern prints," "1:1 frame ratio," and "set of 12 square prints." Listings show sellers offering 8x8/10x10/12x12/16x16/20x20/24x24 square sizes. Reddit thread about printing 12x12 digital downloads at smaller sizes. Gold City Ventures lists 12x12 as one of the top 3 most popular print sizes (along with 10x8 and 10x14).
- **Why it's a gap:** We have ZERO square size content. Our ratio page covers 2:3 vs 4:5 but never mentions 1:1. Square prints are a distinct category — IKEA RIBBA frames are square, gallery walls use square mixes, and Instagram-native sellers default to square. No competitor has a dedicated square print size page for Etsy sellers.
- **ICP fit:** Perfect. Sellers creating gallery wall sets, Instagram-repurposed art, or modern minimalist prints need square sizes. SnapToSize handles 1:1 crops. This fills a ratio gap AND a size gap simultaneously.
- **Suggested slug:** /etsy-square-print-sizes
- **Page type:** size/niche hybrid (1:1 ratio guide with 8x8, 10x10, 12x12 specifics)
- **Priority:** HIGH — large active market, completely uncovered ratio, fills a hole in our size matrix

### 3. Etsy Listing Photo Size Guide (Mockup/Thumbnail Dimensions)
- **Search evidence:** Etsy help article is #1 for listing image queries. Reddit (r/EtsySellers) thread "I'm so confused about listing image sizing..." — sellers confuse LISTING photo dimensions with PRINT file dimensions. Multiple YouTube tutorials (bykristinanicole, others) get 100K+ views. Canva has a dedicated "Etsy size guide" page for listing images. Printify, Closo, Nifty.ai, and Ailee.ai all have guides. Facebook groups asking about mockup creation.
- **Why it's a gap:** We cover print file sizes but NOT listing photo sizes. Sellers searching "etsy image size" or "etsy photo dimensions" land on listing-photo guides, not print-file guides. We could capture this adjacent traffic and funnel it to our print-size content. Many sellers confuse the two — a page clarifying "listing photos vs print files" would be a natural bridge.
- **ICP fit:** Good — every Etsy digital download seller needs both listing photos AND print files. The confusion between the two is a real pain point. However, this is slightly outside our core product (we resize print files, not listing photos). Still a strong traffic play with clear internal linking to our core pages.
- **Suggested slug:** /etsy-listing-photo-size
- **Page type:** guide
- **Priority:** MEDIUM-HIGH — high search volume, adjacent to our ICP, good funnel page but doesn't directly showcase SnapToSize

### 4. Etsy Abstract Wall Art Sizes
- **Search evidence:** "Printable wall art abstract" is a major Etsy marketplace category. "Abstract wall art" shows PostArtsy offering 4-size bundles at $9. "Printable art abstract set" is another active category. Etsy sellers are actively selling abstract printable sets. One listing shows "Includes 4 Sizes" as a selling point.
- **Why it's a gap:** We have botanical, nursery, gallery wall, quote, and minimalist niche pages. No abstract art page. Abstract is arguably the LARGEST niche for digital download wall art on Etsy — it appeals to the broadest buyer base and is the easiest to create (especially with AI tools).
- **ICP fit:** Strong. Abstract art sellers need to know which sizes to offer. They often create in one size and need to resize to many — exactly what SnapToSize does. The AI art angle (Midjourney/DALL-E) makes this even more relevant for newer sellers.
- **Suggested slug:** /etsy-abstract-wall-art-sizes
- **Page type:** niche
- **Priority:** MEDIUM-HIGH — large niche, follows our proven niche page template exactly

### 5. How to Prepare AI Art for Etsy (Midjourney/DALL-E Print Sizes)
- **Search evidence:** Reddit thread "Obviously AI generated art getting tons of sales" shows this is a hot topic. YouTube video "How to Prepare AI Art as Digital Files for Etsy" has significant views. BulkMockup has a page "6 Best AI Wall Art Generators For Etsy Sellers." Active Etsy marketplace for "AI Midjourney art" and "how to sell AI art." TikTok trending topic "best digital downloads to sell Etsy 2025."
- **Why it's a gap:** AI-generated art is a massive growth wave on Etsy. Sellers using Midjourney/DALL-E generate images at non-standard sizes (1024x1024, 1024x1792, etc.) that need resizing to standard print sizes. Nobody has a definitive "AI art to Etsy print sizes" conversion guide. BulkMockup mentions AI briefly but doesn't have a dedicated page.
- **ICP fit:** Excellent. AI art sellers are the fastest-growing segment of Etsy print sellers. They generate one image and need it in 8-15 print sizes — SnapToSize's exact use case. They're also less design-savvy, so more likely to need a tool vs. doing it manually.
- **Suggested slug:** /ai-art-etsy-print-sizes
- **Page type:** guide
- **Priority:** MEDIUM — growing audience, but keyword competition may be lower; risk of Etsy policy changes on AI art

### 6. Etsy Photography Print Sizes (Photo Prints vs Art Prints)
- **Search evidence:** Active Etsy marketplace categories for "digital landscape photo prints" and "landscape photo digital download." Reddit thread about landscape photography print ratios. Facebook post asking about photographic digital artwork file sizes. Photography prints have different ratio expectations than art prints (3:2 from camera sensors vs 4:5 from art tradition).
- **Why it's a gap:** Photography sellers face a unique challenge — their source images are typically 3:2 (from camera sensors) and they need to offer sizes in both 3:2 AND 4:5 ratios, which requires cropping. No page addresses this photography-specific workflow.
- **ICP fit:** Good. Photography sellers are a subset of our ICP. They need batch resizing AND intelligent cropping across ratios. Overlaps well with landscape orientation content.
- **Suggested slug:** /etsy-photography-print-sizes
- **Page type:** niche/guide hybrid
- **Priority:** MEDIUM — could be combined with landscape orientation page or stand alone

- **Etsy digital download SEO tags/titles** — High search volume but completely outside our product domain. We're a resize tool, not an SEO tool. Would dilute topical authority.
- **Etsy planner sizes (half letter, etc.)** — Active market but planners are a different product category than wall art. Different ICP (planner creators vs. wall art sellers). Would require new template and sizing data we don't cover.
- **Vintage poster print sizes** — Reddit thread asking about poster sizes (A4/A3/A2). Our existing A3, A4, 24x36, 18x24 pages already cover these sizes. No unique angle for "vintage" specifically.
- **How to create Etsy listing mockups** — Huge search volume and seller demand, but completely outside our product. We resize print files, not listing photos. Better left to Canva/BulkMockup/mockup template sellers.

## Firecrawl Evaluation

- **Quality of results vs WebSearch:** SAME to SLIGHTLY BETTER. Firecrawl search returns cleaner structured results with title/description/URL. The search operators (site:, quotes) work well. Main advantage: consistent JSON format makes it easy to process. Disadvantage: no snippets as rich as Google's featured snippets.
- **Most valuable use:** Broad topic discovery searches were excellent. Finding Reddit threads, competitor pages, and Etsy marketplace categories in a single query was efficient. The search quality is comparable to Google for this type of research.
- **Worth $16/mo upgrade:** NEED MORE DATA. For this research task, the free tier (500 credits) was more than sufficient — used only ~19 credits. The scrape functionality (which we didn't need today) would be the real value-add for deeper competitor analysis. Verdict: keep on free tier, evaluate after 2-3 more research sessions.
