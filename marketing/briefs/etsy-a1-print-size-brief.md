## Product Data (from CONTENT_REFERENCE.md)

- **A1 dimensions:** 594 x 841 mm = 23.39 x 33.11 inches
- **Pixels at 300 DPI:** 7016 x 9933
- **Aspect ratio:** 1:1.414 (ISO standard, same as all A-sizes)
- **Pack membership:** ISO A-Series pack (A5, A4, A3, A2, A1)
- **Orientation:** Portrait (width < height)
- **A1 is the LARGEST size in the ISO pack** — A0 is single export only, NOT in the pack
- **Output format:** JPEG, 300 DPI, Lanczos resampling, stretch-only (no crop)

## FAQ Topics (6 questions)

1. **"What is A1 size in inches?"** — 23.39 x 33.11 inches (594 x 841 mm). Larger than A2 but slightly smaller than the US 24x36 poster size.

2. **"What is A1 size in pixels at 300 DPI?"** — 7016 x 9933 pixels. This is the minimum resolution for sharp, print-quality A1 output.

3. **"Is A1 the same as 24x36?"** — No. A1 is 23.39x33.11 inches (1:1.414 ratio), while 24x36 is exactly 24x36 inches (2:3 ratio). They are close in physical size but different proportions — offer both to cover international and US buyers.

4. **"Do Etsy buyers actually want A1 prints?"** — Yes. Etsy has dedicated marketplace categories for A1 poster art. A1 is a standard large-format size in the UK, Europe, and Australia, popular for living room statement pieces and office art.

5. **"Where can buyers find A1 frames?"** — IKEA LOMVIKEN series (market dependent), Amazon (search "A1 poster frame" or "594x841mm frame"), and custom framing shops. A1 clip frames are widely available and affordable. Include frame sourcing info in your Etsy listing description.

6. **"Should I offer A1 alongside US sizes?"** — Yes. Including ISO sizes (A5 through A1) alongside US sizes maximizes your international reach. All A-series sizes share the same 1:1.414 ratio, so one design scales across all of them without cropping. SnapToSize generates both ISO and US sizes from one upload.
