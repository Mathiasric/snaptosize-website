## Differentiation Angle

11×17 is the sweet spot between standard letter size (8.5×11) and large-format posters (18×24, 24×36). It is the largest size most home printers can handle (wide-format inkjets), making it the go-to for craft show sellers and buyers who want to print at home without visiting a print shop. It is also often confused with A3 — this page should clarify the difference and explain why sellers should offer both.

### Section 2: What Is 11×17? (The Tabloid Size Explained)
- Physical dimensions: 11×17 inches = 279.4×431.8 mm
- Also known as: Tabloid (portrait), Ledger (landscape), ANSI B
- Exactly twice US Letter (8.5×11) — two Letter sheets side by side = one Ledger sheet
- Common use cases: small posters, craft show displays, home printing on wide-format inkjets, event flyers, menus
- Why Etsy buyers want it: largest size for home printing without a professional print shop

### Section 3: Exact Pixel Dimensions at 300 DPI
- 11×17 at 300 DPI = 3300×5100 pixels
- Quick reference table:

| DPI | Pixel Dimensions | Use Case |
|-----|-----------------|----------|
| 300 | 3300×5100 | Professional print quality (required for Etsy) |
| 150 | 1650×2550 | Draft / screen preview only |

- Source file minimum: 3300×5100 pixels or larger to maintain 300 DPI at full size

### Section 4: 11×17 vs A3 — Know the Difference
- Side-by-side comparison table:

| Attribute | 11×17 (Tabloid) | A3 (ISO) |
|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| Inches | 11×17 | 11.69×16.54 |
| mm | 279.4×431.8 | 297×420 |
| Pixels @300 DPI | 3300×5100 | 3508×4961 |
| Aspect ratio | ~1:1.545 | 1:1.414 (√2) |
| Standard | ANSI (US) | ISO 216 (international) |
| Common in | USA, Canada | Europe, Asia, Australia |

- They are NOT interchangeable — different aspect ratios mean resizing one to the other requires cropping or white bars
- Recommendation: offer both 11×17 AND A3 to cover US and international buyers

### Section 5: Framing Guide for 11×17 Prints
- Standard frame: 11×17 ready-made frames (widely available at craft stores, IKEA, Amazon)
- With mat: Common to use a 16×20 frame with a mat opening cut to 11×17 — adds 2-3 inches of border
- Matting elevates the presentation and is popular for craft show displays
- Listing tip: mention framing options in the Etsy description so buyers know how to display
- Note: 11×17 frames are more available in the US than internationally (where A3 frames dominate)

### Section 8: FAQ (5-8 questions)
1. **What are the pixel dimensions for 11×17 at 300 DPI?**
   → 3300×5100 pixels in portrait orientation (5100×3300 in landscape).

2. **Is 11×17 the same as A3?**
   → No. A3 is 11.69×16.54 inches (297×420mm) while 11×17 is exactly 11×17 inches (279.4×431.8mm). They have different aspect ratios and cannot be used interchangeably without cropping.

3. **What frame size do I need for an 11×17 print?**
   → An 11×17 frame without matting, or a 16×20 frame with a mat cut to 11×17 for a bordered presentation.

4. **Is 11×17 a good size for Etsy digital downloads?**
   → Yes. It is the largest size most buyers can print at home on a wide-format inkjet, making it popular for poster art, craft show displays, and home decor. It is also widely available as a ready-made frame size in the US.

5. **What ratio pack does 11×17 belong to in SnapToSize?**
   → 11×17 is in the Extras pack alongside 5×7, 8.5×11, 11×14, 13×19, and 20×24. Its unique ~11:17 ratio does not fit the standard 2:3, 3:4, 4:5, or ISO packs.

6. **Should I offer both 11×17 and A3 in my listings?**
   → Yes. 11×17 serves US buyers and A3 serves international buyers. They are close in size but not identical, so offering both maximizes your market reach.

7. **Can buyers print 11×17 at home?**
   → Yes, with a wide-format inkjet printer (many consumer models support tabloid/11×17). This is one of the reasons it is popular — buyers do not need to visit a print shop.
