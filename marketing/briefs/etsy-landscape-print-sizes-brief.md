1. **"What is the most popular landscape print size?"** вЂ” 10Г—8 (landscape version of 8Г—10) is the most popular horizontal print size, followed by 14Г—11 and 20Г—16. These match the most common frame sizes available in stores.

3. **"Should I offer both landscape and portrait versions of my Etsy prints?"** вЂ” Yes, offering both orientations effectively doubles your catalog and serves buyers who need art for different wall spaces. Some rooms (above sofas, beds) naturally call for horizontal art. SnapToSize generates both from a single upload.

4. **"What pixel dimensions do I need for landscape prints at 300 DPI?"** вЂ” Swap the portrait dimensions. For example, an 8Г—10 portrait at 300 DPI is 2400Г—3000 pixels; the landscape 10Г—8 version is 3000Г—2400 pixels. The total pixel count is identical.

5. **"Can buyers find frames for landscape prints?"** вЂ” Yes. Standard frames like 8Г—10, 11Г—14, and 16Г—20 can be used in either orientation. Most retailers stock frames that work both ways. Just ensure your listing clearly states the orientation.

6. **"How do I label landscape prints in my Etsy listings?"** вЂ” Include "Landscape" or "Horizontal" in your title and tags. List the dimensions as width Г— height (e.g., "10Г—8" not "8Г—10") and state "Landscape / Horizontal orientation" in the description. Include a mockup showing the art in a horizontal frame.

7. **"What's the difference between landscape and panoramic prints?"** вЂ” Landscape prints use standard aspect ratios (2:3, 3:4, 4:5) in horizontal orientation. Panoramic prints use wider ratios (1:2, 1:3) for extra-wide compositions. Standard landscape sizes fit standard frames; panoramic sizes require specialty framing.
