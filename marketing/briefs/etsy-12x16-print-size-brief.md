1. **What pixel dimensions do I need for a 12×16 print at 300 DPI?**
   - Answer: 3600×4800 pixels exactly. Calculation: 12×300=3600, 16×300=4800. Typical JPEG file size: 6-10 MB.

2. **What aspect ratio is 12×16?**
   - Answer: 3:4 (0.750 decimal). This is the same ratio as 6×8, 9×12, 18×24, 24×32, and 30×40. It also matches the default camera ratio on most smartphones.

3. **Is 12×16 a standard frame size?**
   - Answer: It's standard for canvas prints and widely available online (Amazon, specialty frame shops). In metric markets, 30×40 cm frames are a standard size at IKEA and other retailers. It's less common than 11×14 in US big-box stores, but not rare.

4. **Should I include 12×16 or 11×14 in my Etsy listing?**
   - Answer: Ideally both. They serve different aspect ratios (3:4 vs 11:14), so they cover different buyer needs. 11×14 has better US frame availability; 12×16 captures the 3:4 ratio market and international buyers looking for 30×40 cm prints.

5. **What is 12×16 inches in centimeters?**
   - Answer: 30.5 × 40.6 cm, which maps to the standard 30×40 cm metric frame size. Adding "30×40 cm" to your Etsy listing tags helps international buyers find your prints.
