**Question:** What are the best sizes for celestial wall art on Etsy?

**Answer:**

## FAQ topics (6 questions)

1. **What size should moon phase prints be?** — 8×10 and 5×7 are the top sellers for individual moon prints; 3-piece sets at matching sizes (e.g., three 5×7s or three 8×10s) sell well as gallery walls.
2. **What's the best size for a zodiac wheel print?** — Square formats (8×8, 10×10) suit circular designs; rectangular posters (16×20, 18×24) work when the zodiac is vertically composed.
3. **Should I offer A4 for celestial art?** — Yes. The EU astrology/crystals audience is large; A4 and A3 at 300 DPI are essential for international buyers.
4. **How do I size constellation prints for Etsy?** — 2:3 portrait (4×6, 8×12, 12×18) fits narrow vertical constellations best; landscape (12×16 or 18×24) suits wide-field star maps.
5. **What's a good size for a birth chart print?** — 16×20 and 18×24 allow enough detail for a full birth chart; 12×16 works for simplified versions.
6. **How many sizes should I include in a celestial art listing?** — At minimum: one 2:3 portrait, one 4:5, and A4. Offering all ratios (up to 70 files) eliminates buyer size questions and reduces cart abandonment.
