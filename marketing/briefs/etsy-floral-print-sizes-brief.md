## QuickAnswer box

**Question:** What print sizes should I offer for floral wall art on Etsy?
**Answer:** The core floral sizes are 5×7, 8×10, 11×14, and 16×20. Romantic sub-niches (peonies, roses) over-index on 8×10 and 11×14. Wildflower and garden art sells in tall 2:3 formats (8×12, 12×18). Vintage florals perform well in A4 and A3 for European buyers. Use all 5 ratio packs to cover every buyer's frame.

## FAQ topics (7 questions)

1. **What is the best-selling floral print size on Etsy?** — 8×10 is #1 overall, but floral art specifically over-indexes on 11×14 and 16×20 because buyers use floral prints as bedroom and living room focal points.

2. **Should floral prints be portrait or landscape orientation?** — Portrait (vertical) dominates floral art — roses, wildflower stems, and botanical compositions are naturally tall. Always offer portrait as primary; landscape as secondary for buyers who want horizontal art above sofas.

3. **What size should I use for a rose print on Etsy?** — 8×10 and 11×14 are the most popular frames for rose art. If your composition shows a full stem or arrangement, also offer 8×12 and 12×18 in the 2:3 ratio.

4. **Do wildflower prints need different sizes than rose or peony prints?** — Yes. Wildflower illustrations typically suit tall 2:3 formats (8×12, 12×18, 16×24) because of vertical stem compositions. Rose/peony art fits better in 4:5 (8×10, 16×20) and 3:4 (12×16) formats.

5. **Should I offer A4 for floral prints?** — Yes, especially for vintage floral and pressed flower art. European buyers (UK, Germany, France, Netherlands) use A4 and A3 frames. The ISO pack covers all A-series sizes.

6. **How many sizes should a floral print listing include?** — Aim for 20-30+ sizes across all major ratios. Buyers filter by frame they already own — the more sizes you cover, the more buyers you match.

7. **Can I offer square floral prints on Etsy?** — Yes. Single-stem and abstract floral compositions work well as squares (5×5, 8×8). These sizes are available via Quick Export, not in the ratio packs.
