## FAQ topics (5 questions)

1. **What is the best-selling botanical print size on Etsy?**
   Answer: 8x10 is the #1 seller overall, but botanical art over-indexes on large formats — 16x20 and 24x36 outsell their generic averages because buyers use botanical prints as statement wall pieces. Offering both small and large sizes captures the full market.

2. **How many sizes should I include in a botanical print listing?**
   Answer: Top botanical sellers offer 8-15 sizes per listing. At minimum, include 8x10, 11x14, 16x20, 24x36, and A4. More sizes = higher perceived value, fewer "do you have this in..." messages, and better search visibility on Etsy.

3. **Should botanical prints be vertical or horizontal?**
   Answer: 85%+ of botanical prints on Etsy are vertical (portrait orientation). This follows the natural growth pattern of plants and matches the classic botanical illustration tradition. However, offering both orientations as separate listings can double your catalog with minimal extra work — SnapToSize handles both from the same upload.

4. **What sizes work best for botanical gallery wall sets?**
   Answer: The most popular gallery wall sizes for botanical sets are 8x10, 11x14, and 12x16. All three prints in a set should be the same size for a cohesive look. Offering the set in 3 size options (small/medium/large) in a single listing maximizes conversion.

5. **Do I need to include international (A-series) sizes for botanical prints?**
   Answer: Yes. Botanical art has a strong international buyer base, especially in the UK, Europe, and Australia where A4 and A3 are the standard frame sizes. Skipping A-series means leaving 20-30% of potential buyers without a usable file. SnapToSize's ISO pack generates A5 through A0 automatically.
