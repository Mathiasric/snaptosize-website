## Sub-styles to Cover

1. **Beach Photography** — aerial drone shots, golden hour beach scenes, wave close-ups. Heavily landscape orientation. Best in 2:3 ratio (12x18, 16x24, 20x30). Large format sellers do well here.
2. **Ocean Watercolor** — abstract ocean waves, watercolor seascapes, blue-teal gradients. Works in both orientations. 3:4 and 4:5 ratios popular. Statement pieces at 18x24 and 16x20.
3. **Nautical Illustration** — anchors, lighthouses, ship wheels, knot diagrams, compass roses. Often portrait orientation. 4:5 ratio (8x10, 16x20) and 5x7 for small bathroom/entryway decor.
4. **Tropical Coastal** — palm trees, tropical beaches, monstera + ocean combos, sunset scenes. Landscape and portrait. 2:3 dominant. Gallery wall sets very popular (3-piece or 6-piece).
5. **Coastal Abstract** — minimalist ocean line art, abstract wave forms, sand/sea color blocks. Works in all orientations. 4:5 (8x10, 16x20) for modern/minimalist interiors.
6. **Shell & Marine Life** — seashell collections, coral, starfish, seahorse illustrations. Mostly portrait. 4:5 and 3:4 ratios. Sets of 3-4 matching pieces are a top seller.
7. **Surf Art** — surfboard illustrations, wave photography, surf culture typography. Portrait orientation dominant. 2:3 (12x18, 16x24) and 11x14 popular.

### Ratio pack priority for coastal art:
1. **2:3 ratio** — the coastal hero ratio. Beach panoramas, aerial photography, tropical sunsets all naturally fit 2:3. This ratio includes the most popular large sizes (16x24, 20x30).
2. **4:5 ratio** — essential for the 8x10 and 16x20 workhorses.
3. **3:4 ratio** — strong for watercolors and gallery wall coordination (18x24).
