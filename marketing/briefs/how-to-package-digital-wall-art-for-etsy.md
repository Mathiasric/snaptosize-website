## FAQ Topics (5 questions)

1. **Should I include PNG or JPEG files in my Etsy digital download?**
   Answer: JPEG at 300 DPI. PNG files are 10-20x larger with no quality improvement for prints, and they often exceed Etsy's 20MB limit.

2. **How many sizes should I include in a digital wall art download?**
   Answer: 20-30 sizes across 5 ratio packs (2:3, 3:4, 4:5, ISO, extras) to cover every common frame size buyers own.

3. **What file naming format is best for Etsy digital downloads?**
   Answer: Use descriptive names with dimensions and resolution, like "artwork_12x18_300dpi.jpg" — not generic names like "image1.jpg".

4. **How do I organize files to stay under Etsy's 20MB limit?**
   Answer: Create separate ZIP files for each ratio pack (2:3 sizes in one ZIP, 3:4 in another, etc.) rather than one giant ZIP.

5. **What should I include in my digital download besides the print files?**
   Answer: Include a README.txt or PDF with printing instructions, recommended paper types, and licensing terms.
