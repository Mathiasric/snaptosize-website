## 7. FAQ Topics (6 Questions)

1. **What are the exact pixel dimensions for a 20×30 print at 300 DPI?**
   - Answer: 6000×9000 pixels. Formula: 20×300 = 6000 pixels wide, 30×300 = 9000 pixels tall. This is the standard for professional print quality.

2. **What aspect ratio is 20×30?**
   - Answer: 2:3 (0.667 decimal). Same ratio as 4×6, 6×9, 8×12, 10×15, 12×18, and 16×24. All 7 sizes scale from the same source image without cropping.

3. **Does 20×30 fit a standard frame?**
   - Answer: Yes. 20×30 is a standard poster frame size available at Target, Walmart, IKEA, Michaels, and Amazon. Buyers can also mat a 20×30 print inside a 24×36 frame for a gallery look.

4. **Should I include 20×30 or 24×36 in my Etsy listing?**
   - Answer: Both. 20×30 is included automatically in the 2:3 ratio pack (7 sizes). 24×36 is available via single export. Together they cover the full range of large-format wall art demand.

5. **What file format should I use for 20×30 prints?**
   - Answer: JPEG at 300 DPI with sRGB color profile. PNG at 6000×9000 creates very large files that risk exceeding Etsy's 20MB upload limit. JPEG is compatible with every print service.

6. **How large does my source image need to be for 20×30 at 300 DPI?**
   - Answer: At least 6000×9000 pixels (54 megapixels). Most professional design tools (Photoshop, Illustrator, Procreate) can export at this resolution. If your source is smaller, the print will lose sharpness at full size.
