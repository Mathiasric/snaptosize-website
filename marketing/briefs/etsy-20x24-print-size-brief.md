## FAQ Topics (6 questions)

1. **What are the pixel dimensions for a 20×24 print at 300 DPI?**
   → 6000×7200 pixels (20 inches × 300 = 6000, 24 inches × 300 = 7200)

2. **What aspect ratio is 20×24?**
   → 5:6. This is unique — not the same as 4:5 (16×20, 8×10) or 3:4 (18×24). Requires its own file.

3. **Can I frame a 20×24 print in a standard frame?**
   → Yes. 20×24 frames are widely available at IKEA, Michaels, Amazon, and Target. It's also the standard mat opening for 16×20 prints matted in a larger frame.

4. **What's the difference between 20×24 and 18×24?**
   → Different aspect ratios: 20×24 is 5:6, while 18×24 is 3:4. Both are large format but require separate files — you can't resize one to the other without cropping or distortion.

5. **How large should my source image be for 20×24 at 300 DPI?**
   → At least 6000×7200 pixels. If your source is smaller, the output may appear soft when printed at full size.

6. **Is 20×24 a popular size for Etsy wall art?**
   → Yes. It's a popular large format choice for statement art, gallery presentations, and premium listings. Offering it alongside standard sizes like 8×10 and 16×20 signals completeness to buyers.
