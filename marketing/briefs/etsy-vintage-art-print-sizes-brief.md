### H2 1: Why Vintage Art Needs Its Own Sizing Strategy on Etsy
Original vintage posters used non-standard dimensions: US one-sheet travel posters were 25x40", European art deco posters used DIN A1 (23.4x33.1"), and vintage movie posters were 27x41". Modern buyers want these designs but need them in standard frame sizes (8x10, 16x20, 24x36). This creates a unique challenge: preserving the authentic vintage feel while fitting modern frames. Explain why vintage sellers need more sizes than other niches — buyers are matching vintage art to existing frames, not buying frames to fit the art.

### H2 2: Best-Selling Sizes for Vintage Art Prints on Etsy
Size table organized by buyer use case: small accent (5x7, 8x10), mid-size popular (11x14, 12x16), large statement/poster (16x20, 18x24), full poster (24x36, 20x30), international (A5, A4, A3, A2, A1). Note that vintage art over-indexes on larger poster sizes — 18x24 and 24x36 are the sweet spot because vintage posters are historically large-format. Also note 11x17 as a standout for vintage: it's tabloid/ledger format that echoes original small-format vintage ads. Include pixel dimensions at 300 DPI.

### H2 3: Sizing by Vintage Art Sub-Style
Break down sizing recommendations by sub-style:
- **Art Deco (1920s-1930s)** — Tall, narrow compositions. 2:3 ratio is ideal (12x18, 16x24, 24x36). 11x17 for smaller prints. Gold/black color schemes shine large. A2 and A1 for EU market.
- **Mid-Century Modern (1950s-1960s)** — Clean geometry, bold colors. 4:5 ratio works well (8x10, 16x20). Square formats popular. 18x24 is the sweet spot.
- **Retro Travel Posters** — Vertical orientation dominant. 2:3 ratio (matches original travel poster proportions). 18x24 and 24x36 are top sellers. 13x19 for fine art prints.
- **Vintage Botanical Illustrations** — Portrait 3:4 and 4:5 ratios. Mid-sizes sell best (8x10, 11x14, 12x16). A4 and A3 for international (botanical art is huge in UK/EU).
- **Antique Maps & Charts** — Landscape orientation often needed. Larger sizes (16x20+, 18x24, 20x30). Custom ratios common — offer both orientations.
- **Retro Kitchen / Advertising Art** — Smaller accent sizes (5x7, 8x10, 8.5x11). US Letter for home printing. Fun, casual framing.
- **Art Nouveau (1890s-1910s)** — Tall, flowing compositions. 2:3 portrait dominant. 11x17 echoes original poster format. A-series sizes essential (style is European-origin).

## FAQ topics (6 questions)

1. **What are the most popular print sizes for vintage art on Etsy?** — 18x24 and 24x36 lead for vintage poster styles (art deco, retro travel). 8x10 and 11x14 are top sellers for vintage botanical and retro kitchen art. 11x17 is uniquely popular for vintage art because it echoes original small-format poster dimensions. Include A3 and A2 for international buyers.

2. **What size were original vintage posters?** — Original US travel/movie posters were 25x40" or 27x41". European art deco posters used DIN A1 (23.4x33.1"). Window cards were 14x22". Modern reprints and digital downloads are resized to standard frame sizes — 24x36" is the closest standard match for full-size vintage posters.

3. **Should vintage art prints be portrait or landscape orientation?** — Most vintage art is portrait orientation — travel posters, art deco, art nouveau, and botanical illustrations were all designed tall and narrow. Antique maps and some mid-century modern pieces work in landscape. Offering both orientations doubles your listing value and covers more buyer needs.

4. **How many sizes should I include in a vintage art listing on Etsy?** — Top-selling vintage art shops offer 15+ sizes per listing across multiple ratios (2:3, 3:4, 4:5, ISO A-series, and extras like 11x17). More sizes mean more buyers who find a match for their existing frame. Gallery wall sets should have matching sizes across all pieces.

5. **Do I need international sizes for vintage art prints?** — Yes. Art deco, art nouveau, and vintage botanical styles originated in Europe. A4, A3, A2, and A1 are standard in the UK, EU, and Australia. Omitting A-series sizes means losing buyers in markets where vintage art is especially popular.

6. **What's the best ratio for vintage-style art prints?** — 2:3 is the most authentic vintage ratio — it closely matches original poster proportions. 4:5 works well for mid-century modern and smaller vintage pieces. 3:4 suits vintage botanical illustrations. Offering all three ratios covers the full range of vintage sub-styles.
