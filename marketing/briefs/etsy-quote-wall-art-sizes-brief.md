### H2 5: Typography-Specific Sizing Considerations
- **Margins and breathing room:** Text art needs more white space than photographic art. At small sizes (4x6, 5x7), ensure your source design has adequate margins so text doesn't feel cramped.
- **Readability at scale:** A quote that's legible at 24x36 may have too-small text at 5x7. Design tip: test your quote at 5x7 — if it's readable there, it works everywhere.
- **Ratio stretching and text distortion:** Different ratios stretch artwork differently. SnapToSize uses stretch-only resize (no cropping), so text proportions change slightly across ratios. For quote art, start with a balanced composition that tolerates mild ratio variation.
- **Font weight matters:** Thin/light fonts can look washed out at small sizes and low print quality. Use medium to bold weights for quote art that prints at any size.
- **Color considerations:** Dark text on light backgrounds is most forgiving across print services. Reverse (light text on dark) uses more ink and can vary between printers.

## FAQ Topics (7 questions)

1. **What is the best size for quote wall art on Etsy?** — 8x10 is the best-selling single size for quote art, but top sellers include all 5 ratio packs (30+ sizes) to capture every buyer preference. The more sizes you offer, the more search terms your listing matches.

2. **How many sizes should I include in a quote art listing?** — Include at least 8-10 sizes across multiple ratios. Professional sellers offer 30+ sizes covering 2:3, 3:4, 4:5, ISO, and Extras packs. More sizes = higher perceived value = higher price point.

3. **Do I need international sizes (A4, A3) for quote art?** — Yes. Quote art has strong international demand, especially in the UK, Europe, and Australia where A-series paper is standard. Including ISO sizes (A5 through A1) opens your listing to global buyers.

4. **What resolution should quote wall art be for Etsy?** — 300 DPI is the professional print standard. For an 8x10 print, that means 2400x3000 pixels. Higher resolution source files produce better results across all sizes.

5. **Should I include square sizes for quote art?** — Yes. Square formats (8x8, 10x10, 12x12) are popular for quote art in Instagram-style gallery walls and modern interiors. Square sizes are available via Quick Export.

6. **How do I prevent text from looking distorted across different ratios?** — Design your quote with generous margins and center the text. SnapToSize uses stretch-only resize (no cropping), which preserves the full composition. Starting with a well-centered design ensures readability across all ratios.

7. **Can I resize quote art without losing text quality?** — Yes, as long as your source file is high resolution (4000+ pixels on the longest side). SnapToSize uses Lanczos resampling at 300 DPI — the highest quality resize algorithm — so text stays crisp at every output size.
