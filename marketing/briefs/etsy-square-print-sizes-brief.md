FAQ topics (6 questions):

1. **What are the standard square print sizes for Etsy?** — The 8 standard square sizes are 5x5, 8x8, 10x10, 12x12, 16x16, 18x18, 20x20, and 24x24 inches. All at 300 DPI for professional print quality.

2. **What is the most popular square print size on Etsy?** — 12x12 is the most popular, followed by 8x8 and 10x10. 12x12 fits standard square frames and is large enough for a statement piece without dominating a wall.

3. **Should I include square sizes in my Etsy printable listings?** — Yes. Square prints are trending for modern interiors and gallery walls. Adding square sizes alongside your rectangular offerings increases perceived value, justifies higher pricing, and captures buyers who specifically search for square art.

4. **What pixel dimensions do I need for square prints at 300 DPI?** — Multiply inches by 300. Examples: 8x8 = 2400x2400 pixels, 12x12 = 3600x3600 pixels, 20x20 = 6000x6000 pixels.

5. **Do square prints need different source images than rectangular prints?** — Your source image should be at least as large as your biggest square size (24x24 = 7200x7200 pixels at 300 DPI). Square formats work best with centered compositions since the 1:1 ratio has equal visual weight on all sides.

6. **Can I mix square and rectangular prints in a gallery wall set?** — Yes. Mixing square and rectangular prints creates visual interest. A common arrangement is one large square (12x12 or 16x16) flanked by smaller rectangular prints (8x10, 5x7). Include both formats in your listing for maximum appeal.
