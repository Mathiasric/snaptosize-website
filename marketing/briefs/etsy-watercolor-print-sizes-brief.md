## FAQ topics (7 questions)

1. **What are the most popular print sizes for watercolor art on Etsy?** — 8x10, 11x14, and 16x20 are the top three sellers. Including 5x7 for small frames and 24x36 for statement pieces covers the full range.

2. **What resolution should watercolor prints be?** — 300 DPI is the professional print standard. At 300 DPI, an 8x10 print needs 2400x3000 pixels and a 16x20 needs 4800x6000 pixels.

3. **How many sizes should I offer for each watercolor painting?** — The more sizes you offer, the more buyers you reach. Top-selling watercolor shops offer 10+ sizes per listing across multiple ratios (2:3, 3:4, 4:5, ISO, and extras).

4. **Should watercolor prints include international paper sizes?** — Yes. A4 and A3 are standard in the UK, EU, and Australia. Including ISO A-series sizes opens your listings to international buyers.

5. **What file format is best for watercolor digital downloads?** — JPG at 300 DPI is the Etsy standard. It keeps file sizes manageable (under Etsy's 20MB limit per file) while preserving watercolor detail.

6. **Do I need to offer both portrait and landscape orientations?** — Yes. Many watercolor paintings work in both orientations (especially abstracts and landscapes). Offering both doubles the value of each listing.

7. **How do I keep my watercolor ZIP files under Etsy's 20MB limit?** — Organize files by ratio pack instead of putting all sizes in one ZIP. Each ratio pack stays well under 20MB when using optimized JPG at 300 DPI.
