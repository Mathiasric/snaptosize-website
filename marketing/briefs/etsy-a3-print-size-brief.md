## FAQ Questions (from People Also Ask + search analysis)

1. **What is A3 size in inches?**
   A3 is 11.69 x 16.54 inches (297 x 420 mm). Often rounded to 11.7 x 16.5 inches.

2. **How many pixels is A3 at 300 DPI?**
   3508 x 4961 pixels in portrait orientation. This is the standard for print-quality Etsy digital downloads.

3. **Is A3 the same as 11x17?**
   No. A3 (11.69 x 16.54 in) and Tabloid/11x17 (11 x 17 in) are close but not identical. They have different aspect ratios and come from different standards (ISO vs ANSI).

4. **Is A3 bigger than A4?**
   Yes. A3 is exactly double A4 in area. A3 = 297x420mm, A4 = 210x297mm. Folding an A3 sheet in half gives you two A4 sheets.

5. **What is A3 good for on Etsy?**
   A3 is ideal for wall art posters, botanical prints, nursery art, and gallery wall pieces. It is the standard poster size in UK, EU, and Australian markets.

6. **Do I need to offer A3 for Etsy digital downloads?**
   If you sell wall art or posters, yes. A3 is the most popular poster size in international markets. Missing it means losing UK, EU, and Australian buyers.

7. **What frame size do I need for A3?**
   An A3 frame fits 297x420mm prints. IKEA, Amazon, and most frame shops in the UK, EU, and Australia stock A3 frames. In the US, the closest standard frame is 12x18 inches (with matting adjustment).

8. **Can I resize A4 to A3 without losing quality?**
   Upscaling A4 to A3 doubles the area and will cause visible quality loss. Always create your original artwork at A3 resolution (3508x4961px) or larger, then scale down for smaller sizes.
