## FAQ topics (7 questions)

1. **What are the most popular print sizes for boho wall art on Etsy?** — 8x10, 11x14, and 16x20 are the top three sellers. For large statement pieces, 18x24 and 24x36 are popular. Including 5x7 for small frames and A4/A3 for international buyers covers the full range.

2. **How many sizes should I offer for each boho print?** — The more sizes you offer, the more buyers you reach. Top-selling boho art shops offer 15+ sizes per listing across multiple ratios (2:3, 3:4, 4:5, ISO, and extras). Gallery wall sets should have matching sizes across all pieces.

3. **What sizes work best for boho gallery wall sets?** — Offer a mix: one large anchor piece (16x20 or 18x24), medium companions (11x14, 12x16), and smaller fillers (5x7, 8x10). Every piece in the set should be available in the same size options so buyers can choose their preferred arrangement.

4. **Should boho prints include international paper sizes?** — Yes. Boho decor is a global trend. A4 and A3 are standard in the UK, EU, and Australia. Including ISO A-series sizes opens your listings to international buyers who would otherwise skip your shop.

5. **What resolution should boho art prints be?** — 300 DPI is the professional print standard. At 300 DPI, an 8x10 print needs 2400x3000 pixels and a 16x20 needs 4800x6000 pixels. This preserves fine details like pampas grass textures and macrame line work.

6. **Do I need portrait and landscape orientations for boho art?** — Yes. Desert landscapes and horizontal abstracts need landscape orientation. Pampas grass and celestial designs often work in both. Offering both orientations doubles the value and lets gallery wall buyers create asymmetrical arrangements.

7. **How do I keep my boho art ZIP files under Etsy's 20MB limit?** — Organize files by ratio pack instead of putting all sizes in one ZIP. Each ratio pack stays well under 20MB when using optimized JPG at 300 DPI. For gallery wall sets, pack each ratio across all pieces together.
