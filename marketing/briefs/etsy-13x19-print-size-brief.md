## FAQ Topics (6 questions)

1. **What are the pixel dimensions for a 13×19 print at 300 DPI?** — 3900×5700 pixels. This is the minimum resolution for sharp, professional-quality prints.

2. **What aspect ratio is 13×19?** — Approximately 1:1.46 (close to 2:3 but not exact). It's its own unique ratio, which is why SnapToSize includes it in the Extras pack rather than a standard ratio pack.

3. **What frame size fits a 13×19 print?** — An 18×24 frame with a standard mat creates a 2.5-inch border on all sides. Frameless 13×19 frames are available from specialty retailers.

4. **Is 13×19 the same as A3?** — No. A3 is 11.7×16.5 inches (297×420mm). 13×19 is larger — sometimes called A3+ or Super B. Both are popular for fine art, but 13×19 gives about 35% more print area.

5. **Can buyers print 13×19 at home?** — Yes, with a wide-format inkjet printer (13-inch or wider). Models like the Epson SureColor P700 or Canon PIXMA Pro-200 handle 13×19 paper. Mention this in your listing description.

6. **Should I include 13×19 in my Etsy listings?** — Yes, if you target fine art buyers, photographers, or collectors. It's a premium size that signals quality. SnapToSize generates it automatically as part of the Extras pack — no extra work.
