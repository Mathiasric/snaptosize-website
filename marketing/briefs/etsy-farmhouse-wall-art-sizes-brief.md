## FAQ topics (6 questions)

1. **What are the most popular print sizes for farmhouse wall art on Etsy?** — 8x10, 11x14, and 16x20 are the top three sellers. For large statement pieces, 18x24 and 20x30 are popular above sofas and in dining rooms. Including 5x7 for small frames and A4/A3 for international buyers covers the full range.

2. **How many sizes should I offer for each farmhouse print?** — The more sizes you offer, the more buyers you reach. Top-selling farmhouse art shops offer 15+ sizes per listing across multiple ratios (2:3, 3:4, 4:5, ISO, and extras). Gallery wall sets should have matching sizes across all pieces.

3. **What sizes work best for farmhouse gallery wall sets?** — Offer a mix: one large anchor piece (16x20 or 18x24), medium companions (11x14, 12x16), and smaller fillers (5x7, 8x10). Every piece in the set should be available in the same size options so buyers can choose their preferred arrangement. Symmetrical grids work well with matching sizes like 8x10 or 11x14.

4. **Should farmhouse prints include international paper sizes?** — Yes. Farmhouse decor is popular worldwide, especially in the UK, Australia, and continental Europe. A4 and A3 are standard frame sizes in these markets. Including ISO A-series sizes opens your listings to international buyers.

5. **What's the difference between modern farmhouse and rustic farmhouse sizing?** — Modern farmhouse art tends toward medium sizes (8x10, 11x14, 16x20) in symmetrical gallery wall arrangements with clean-lined frames. Rustic farmhouse art often works better at larger sizes (16x24, 20x30) where weathered textures and landscape scenes have more visual impact. Both styles should offer the full range for maximum sales.

6. **What resolution should farmhouse art prints be?** — 300 DPI is the professional print standard. At 300 DPI, an 8x10 print needs 2400x3000 pixels and a 16x20 needs 4800x6000 pixels. This preserves fine details like botanical illustration line work, vintage typography, and weathered texture detail.
