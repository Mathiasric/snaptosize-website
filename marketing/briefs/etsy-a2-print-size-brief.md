## Product Data (from CONTENT_REFERENCE.md)

- **A2 dimensions:** 420 x 594 mm = 16.54 x 23.39 inches
- **Pixels at 300 DPI:** 4961 x 7016
- **Aspect ratio:** 1:1.414 (ISO standard, same as all A-sizes)
- **Pack membership:** ISO A-Series pack (A5, A4, A3, A2, A1)
- **Orientation:** Portrait (width < height) per LESSON-029
- **Output format:** JPEG, 300 DPI, Lanczos resampling, stretch-only (no crop)
- **A0 note:** A0 is single export only, NOT in the ISO pack (LESSON-050)

FAQ topics (7 questions):
- "What is A2 size in inches?" — 16.54 x 23.39 inches (420 x 594 mm). Slightly smaller than 18x24.
- "What is A2 size in pixels at 300 DPI?" — 4961 x 7016 pixels. This is the minimum resolution for sharp A2 prints.
- "Is A2 the same as 18x24?" — No. A2 is 16.54x23.39 (1:1.414 ratio), 18x24 is exactly 18x24 (3:4 ratio). Close in physical size but different proportions. Offer both.
- "Do Etsy buyers actually want A2?" — Yes, especially UK, European, and Australian buyers. A2 is a standard poster/frame size in metric countries.
- "What frames fit A2 prints?" — IKEA RIBBA and LOMVIKEN series, plus most European frame retailers. In the US, search for "16.5x23.4 frame" or "420x594mm frame" on Amazon.
- "How many pixels do I need for an A2 print?" — Minimum 4961 x 7016 pixels for 300 DPI. Your source image should be at least this resolution or larger.
- "Should I offer A2 alongside US sizes?" — Yes. Including ISO sizes (A5 through A1) alongside US sizes maximizes your international reach. SnapToSize generates both from one upload.
