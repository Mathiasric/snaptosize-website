# SnapToSize

**What it does:** Image resizing app that auto-crops photos to all required Etsy listing sizes and aspect ratios, then packages them as a ZIP for instant upload.

**Users:** Etsy sellers (handmade, digital, print-on-demand). Non-technical. Care about their shop looking professional and saving time. Often frustrated by crop failures and rejected uploads.

**Brand voice:** Direct, warm, practical. No hype. No jargon. Speaks like a knowledgeable Etsy seller friend, not a SaaS marketing page.

**Anti-references:** Purple gradients on white. Glassmorphism. "Boost your productivity" copy. Generic floating card grids. Stock photo heroes. Inter font everywhere. Lorem ipsum placeholders.

**Key URLs:** snaptosize.com (marketing), app.snaptosize.com (product)
