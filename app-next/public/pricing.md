# Pricing — SnapToSize

SnapToSize resizes Etsy digital wall art to all standard print sizes in one upload.

## Free

- **Price:** $0/month
- **Exports:** 5 quick exports per day + 2 ratio packs per day
- **Concurrent jobs:** 2
- **Watermark:** Yes (on exported images)
- **Features:** All ratio packs available, 300 DPI output, ZIP under 20MB

## Pro

- **Price:** $11.99/month (billed monthly) or $97/year ($8.08/month, saves 33%)
- **Exports:** Unlimited
- **Concurrent jobs:** 7
- **Watermark:** No
- **Features:** All ratio packs, 300 DPI output, ZIP under 20MB, priority processing, all future packs included

## What's included in both plans

- 5 ratio packs: 2:3, 3:4, 4:5, 5:7, ISO A-series (A4, A3, A2, A1)
- Square pack: 1:1
- Large format: single exports (24×36, 20×30, etc.)
- Up to 28 files per upload
- ZIP file under 20MB (Etsy's upload limit)
- 300 DPI output
- Browser-based — no software install

## Notes

- No credit card required for free plan
- Upgrade/cancel anytime
- No account required to try (start on free plan immediately)

More details: https://snaptosize.com/pricing
