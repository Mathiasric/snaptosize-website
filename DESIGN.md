# SnapToSize Design System

## Colors
- Primary / CTA: `#2DD4BF` (teal) — buttons, conversion elements
- Secondary accent: `#A78BFA` (purple) — mid-content inline CTAs only
- Background: dark (`#0F172A` / `#1E293B`), light sections `#F8FAFC`
- Text: `#F1F5F9` on dark, `#0F172A` on light
- Never: arbitrary gradients, purple-on-white, neon without purpose

## Typography
- Display: expressive, characterful — not Inter, not Roboto, not Arial
- Body: readable at 16px+, high contrast
- Hierarchy: 3 levels max per section
- Never: 6 font sizes on one page, thin weights on dark backgrounds

## Spacing
- 4px base grid. Sections: 80–120px vertical padding desktop
- Cards: 24px internal padding minimum
- Never: cramped mobile layouts, asymmetric card padding

## Layout
- Marketing site: max-width 1200px, 5% horizontal padding mobile
- Asymmetry and overlap encouraged over flat card grids
- Negative space is intentional

## Components
- Buttons: teal fill (primary), outlined (secondary), purple text (inline CTA)
- Cards: subtle border or shadow — never floating glassmorphism
- Badges: small, teal or slate, no emoji spam

## Motion
- Page load: staggered fade-in, 150–300ms delays
- Hover: 150ms transitions on interactive elements
- Never: spin loaders, bounce animations, parallax on marketing pages

## Voice / UX Copy
- Headlines: outcome-first ("Resize once. Upload everywhere.")
- CTAs: specific ("Resize my photos" not "Get started")
- Errors: calm and actionable, never blame the user

## Brand
- Etsy sellers, for Etsy sellers
- Teal = trust + precision. Purple = bonus / pro feature signal
- Photo-first: real product images beat illustrations

## Anti-Patterns
- Generic purple gradient hero
- "All-in-one" / "powerful" / "seamless" marketing copy
- Floating cards with drop shadows on white backgrounds
- Inter + grey-on-white body text
- Stock photo of hands on laptop
- Feature lists without outcome framing
