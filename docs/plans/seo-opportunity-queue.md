# SEO Opportunity Queue

**Created:** 2026-05-27 · **Source:** Firecrawl competitor + keyword + voice-of-customer sweep (raw research in `.firecrawl/seo-research/` + `.firecrawl/pain-research/`).
**Why this exists:** SEO is the #1 growth lever (distribution is the bottleneck — see `docs/STATE.md`). This is the durable, ranked build queue for the SEO pipeline.

**Before building any page:** check `app-next/data/page-registry.json` (these were verified net-new at sweep time, but re-confirm). Pacing: **max 2 pages/day** (quality > volume). Frame all DPI content around **300 DPI** (what we deliver) — never imply 600.

**SERP insight:** No strong programmatic-SEO competitor exists. Most "etsy [size]" terms = YouTube/Etsy-listings/Reddit. Ratio / frame / tool / cross-platform terms have **weak SERPs** (Quora-only, official-docs-only, no purpose-built guide) = winnable fast.

---

## TIER 1 — easy wins (start here; weak SERP + high intent + maps to real seller pain)

1. **"Why 300 DPI isn't enough" / DPI vs pixel dimensions** — maps to the #1 voice-of-customer pain *"blurry despite 300 DPI."* Only a generic 300dpi page exists. Problem page. *Easy.* **← recommended first page.**
2. **Aspect ratio without cropping / distorting** — maps to VOC *"squeeze into 4–5 ratios without distorting my art."* Direct product-value page. *Easy.*
3. **Size-comparison cluster** — `5x7 vs 4x6` (SERP = Quora only), then `8x10 vs 11x14`, `16x20 vs 18x24`, `24x36 vs 20x30`. Pure gaps. Comparison. *Very easy.*
4. **Ratio cluster** — `3:4 ratio explained`, `1:1 square ratio`, `5:7 ratio`. Only `2-3-vs-4-5` exists today. Ratio. *Easy.*
5. **ISO vs US paper sizes** (A-series vs inches; A4 vs US Letter) — recurring seller confusion; maps to VOC international-sizes question. Problem/size. *Easy.*
6. **IKEA Ribba print sizes / what size for a Ribba frame** — SERP = ikea.com product pages only, zero print-prep guide. Buyer + seller intent. Niche/size. *Easy.*
7. **"11x14 doesn't fit standard ratios"** — VOC pain (odd size); conversion/fit page. *Easy.*
8. **Recommended Etsy print-size pack** ("which sizes should I offer") — VOC decision-pain; positions our pack system. *Easy–medium.*

## TIER 2 — adjacency / TAM expansion (beyond the Etsy-print-size niche)

9. **POD / cross-platform size guides** — `printify size guide for digital sellers`, `shopify digital download print sizes`, `gumroad print size guide`, `society6 / redbubble / gelato upload size requirements`. Our multi-export = the universal fit. Medium.
10. **Source-tool conversion** — `procreate to etsy printable`, `affinity / gimp / illustrator for etsy printables`. Intercepts creators at the export step (only Canva + Photoshop comparison pages exist today). Medium.
11. **Poster sizes for Etsy** (`16x24`, `24x36` "poster" cluster) — "poster" absent from registry. Size. Medium.
12. **Greeting card / invitation digital sizes** — only `wedding-printable` exists; "invitation/card" absent. Niche/size. Easy.

## Adjacency themes (the path past the niche ceiling toward $1M ARR)
- **POD / multi-platform seller:** own "[platform] size guide" for every print marketplace.
- **Frame-buyer / consumer side:** IKEA Ribba, Target, Michaels frame-size guides — underserved SERPs, capture buyers + the sellers serving them.
- **Source-tool conversion:** "from [tool] to print-ready Etsy pack."
